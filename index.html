The Bridge

What is it

You get matched with one person from the other company. Over 12 weeks, you have 5 structured sessions together. You teach them something you are genuinely good at, they teach you something you actually want to learn. Both of you prepare, both of you get something concrete out of it. Neither person is the expert, neither is the student.

⇄

You teach and you learn

Sessions alternate direction. You lead some, your pair leads others. Both of you walk away with something you can use.

◎

A real working relationship

Five 60-minute sessions over 12 weeks builds the kind of connection you cannot get from a company event.

✦

A person at the other company you actually know

By September, this person is not a stranger. You already have a working relationship before you meet.

What this is not This is not mentoring. There is no mentor and no mentee. It is not networking, there are no coffees disguised as development. Every session has a learning goal set in advance and a short reflection written afterwards.

Your 5 sessions

Sessions alternate who leads. Leading a session means you pick the topic, prepare something to share, and are responsible for making that hour worth your pair's time. Sessions 1, 3, and 5 are led by the Phiture person. Sessions 2 and 4 by the Precis person.

When you are matched, you both get a shared Google Doc with a simple template. Before each session, whoever is leading writes a one-sentence learning goal. After, whoever was being taught writes three sentences: what I learned, how I will use it, what I still want to understand.

Are you eligible?

For everyone

  • You have been in your current role for at least 6 months, settled enough to have something real to teach
  • You are not planning extended leave between July and September 2026
  • You can commit a minimum of 2 hours per month (sessions + light prep + a short reflection after)
  • Your manager is aware and supportive, they do not need to formally approve, but they should know

If you are at Precis

  • You work in a function with overlap with Phiture: Performance Marketing, Data, Strategy, Creative, Product, or People
  • You have a genuine reason to engage with Phiture specifically, not just a general interest in peer learning
  • You are at Manager level or above, or a specialist with 3+ years in your domain
  • You are applying with your department head's awareness (self-nomination is fine with their endorsement)
Not sure if you qualify? Apply anyway and explain your reasoning in the form. The selection panel will consider context. What disqualifies an application is not seniority or title, it is vague answers: not being able to name a specific skill to teach, or not having a real learning goal.

The Festival

By the time the Precis Festival comes around in September, you and your pair will have spent 5 hours working through real things together. The Festival is a great occasion to finally meet in person. It is not the end of the programme, just a moment within it, and probably the most enjoyable one.

What it will feel like You will already know this person. You will have taught each other something, worked through a real problem together, and had a few good conversations. Meeting them at the Festival will feel like catching up with a colleague, not introducing yourself to a stranger.
  • There is no Bridge Program session at the Festival. No stage moment, no structured activity.
  • Bridge participants will have a small indicator on their badge so you can spot each other easily in the crowd.
  • Find your pair, have a drink, pick up where you left off.
  • If you have not yet finished all your sessions, the Festival is a great time to book the last one.
6

After the programme

Whether the relationship continues is up to you. Some pairs will keep meeting. Some will stay in touch on Slack. Either is good. The formal programme ends after Session 5, but the connection does not have to.

  • All participants join #bridge-alumni on Slack after the programme ends
  • One optional Alumni Hour in November for anyone who wants to reconnect with the wider group
  • If you want to keep your sessions going, great. If not, no pressure, the programme has already done its job.

How to apply

The application takes about 15–20 minutes. Your answers are used both to decide whether you are selected and to match you with the right person. Specific answers lead to better matches. Vague answers lead to weak ones. Read the questions below before you start so you can prepare.
